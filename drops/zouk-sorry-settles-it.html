How the feud works

Three rules. No fence-sitting.

RULE 01

Pick your side

Every bottle you buy is one vote for that flavour. Buy three, vote three times. The Feud Board updates live all weekend.

RULE 02

The loser says sorry

Every full hour a flavour trails, its price drops S$1 — down to a floor of S$39.90. Prices never climb back up, so the underdog is always the bargain.

RULE 03

Winners go clubbing

Feud ends Sunday 11:59PM. Five backers from the winning side win a booth night at Zouk with bottle service of their flavour. Bring your team.

🏆

The winning side takes the club

Five winners drawn from the winning team, each with a booth for four at Zouk Singapore plus a bottle of the champion flavour on ice. The losing side keeps the discount — and honestly? Sorry, not sorry.
