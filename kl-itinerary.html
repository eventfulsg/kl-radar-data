Standing rules, every day

08:00 · Radar

Check the morning scan

The radar scans 21 KL pages at 8:00 (growth-rate rescan 8:45). Anything ≥4× outperformance within 20 min of the day's zone gets swapped in or added as a fourth stop — "my radar says this is going viral in KL today, so we're going."

Shoot · Checklist

Six shots minimum

Morning hook to camera · walking-in shot at each stop · food close-up + first bite · one insight to camera right after the meeting · 2 min of street b-roll · cliffhanger line before bed. Rough-cut and post the same night.

Meetings · Closers

Same three closers

"Who are 2 other people I should meet before the 19th?" · "Can I film a bit of this?" (asked before the camera comes out) · a next step with a date attached, never "let's keep in touch."

House rule

No number, no post

Same rule as the caption cheat sheet: every spot and every caption carries a number — a price in RM, a queue time, a founding year, a height. It's why every card on this page has one.

Weather

August = afternoon storms

Sequence outdoor spots before 15:00 where possible. Wet-weather swaps: REXKL, Islamic Arts Museum, Hutong, Suria KLCC — all indoor, all already on the plan.

Money

Cash + wallet reality

RM300 in small notes for kopitiams, Wanjo and Kampung Baru; Touch 'n Go eWallet for nearly everything else. Cards are for malls only. Grab across the trip ≈ RM250–350 total.
