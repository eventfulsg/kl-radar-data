How a drop works

Same mechanics every time. Small runs, a fixed drop time, no restocks — so a pass means something the moment you're holding it.

STEP 1

Join the waitlist

Phone number in, done. Waitlist members get a 15-minute head start on every drop.

STEP 2

The drop opens

Always Friday 20:00 SGT. You get one link by SMS. The link holds your place for 10 minutes.

STEP 3

Claim your number

Passes are numbered in claim order. Nº 001 goes to the fastest hands in Singapore.

STEP 4

Sold out. See you there.

When the run's gone, it's gone. Your pass is your entry, your credit, and your collectible.
